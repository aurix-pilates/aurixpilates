AURIX PREMIUM EQUIPMENTS

Reformer (with tower)
Strength • Flexibility • Balance

Dynamic full-body training using springs, straps, and tower attachments. Enhances core strength, posture, flexibility, and controlled movement through guided resistance.

Cadillac (Trapeze Table)
Core • Mobility • Stability

Versatile apparatus training with bars, springs, and straps. Improves mobility, core control, flexibility, and full-body strength through precise supported movements.

Wunda Chair
Power • Control • Balance

Compact equipment training that challenges strength, coordination, and core stability. Builds balance, posture, and full-body control through dynamic resistance exercises.
